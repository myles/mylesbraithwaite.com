Steve Gibson has a good overview on what's happening with TrueCrypt right now.

  1. Don't panic the Open Crypto Audit is still taking place.
  2. The Open Source community will most likely take over the project.
  3. One of the original developers, known as David, was contacted. They said it had nothing to do with the audit, but just were tired of developing the software.
