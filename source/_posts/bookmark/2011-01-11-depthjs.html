An interesting project by the MIT Media Lab to have a gesture-based web browsing experience. The have released the project under the AGPL license and you can clone it on GitHub. I can see this being deployed in hospitals and mechanical shops. Where touching a screen or keyboard could contaminate your hands or the screen or keyboard. (via core77)
