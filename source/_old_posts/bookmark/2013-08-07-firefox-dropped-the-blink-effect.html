--- title: Firefox dropped the blink effect date: 2013-08-07 09:48:00 Z layout: post category: bookmark author: myles tags: - css - html - web-development link: url: https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/23.0/releasenotes/ ---

Firefox is removing the HTML blink effect (dropping both the <blink> tag and text-decoration: blink;) in version 24 for their browser.
